Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Block Breaker ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you play our game.

2. Information We Collect

Block Breaker is designed to be privacy-friendly. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Data: Your scores, levels achieved, and gameplay preferences are stored locally on your device only.
  • Audio Preferences: Your sound and music settings are stored locally in your browser.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

3. Local Storage

The game uses your browser's local storage to save:

  • High scores
  • Sound/music preferences
  • Game settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

When distributed through GameSnacks or other platforms, the game may be subject to additional privacy policies:

  • GameSnacks: May collect anonymous gameplay analytics and serve advertisements. See GameSnacks Privacy Policy
  • Google: May collect data as per their privacy policy if integrated with Google services

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our game does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Any cookies used are from third-party platforms (like GameSnacks) if applicable.

6. Children's Privacy

Block Breaker is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we don't collect any personal data at all, the game is safe for children to play.

7. Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can clear your browser data at any time to remove all game-related information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local game data at any time through your browser settings
  • Disable sound/music features
  • Stop playing the game at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

This game can be played worldwide. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.
